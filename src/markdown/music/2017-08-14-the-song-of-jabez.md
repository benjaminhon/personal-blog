author: Paul Baloche
published_on: 2001

Lord that You would bless me indeed   
And enlarge my territory   
That Your hand would be with me   
That You would keep my heart from evil   
Amen   

Bless me Lord   
Bless me Lord   
So that I might be   
A blessing to this world
